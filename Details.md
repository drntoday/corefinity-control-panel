## Project Goal

We are looking to **fully redesign and modernize our control panel**.

### The new design should be:
- Simple and intuitive  
- UX/UI friendly  
- Clean, modern, and professional  
- Easy to navigate  
- Suitable for a technical hosting platform without being overly complex  

We want a designer who can rethink the experience so it feels:

- More polished  
- More user-friendly  
- More aligned with modern SaaS/cloud products  

The panel should help users complete tasks efficiently, with a strong focus on:

- Clarity  
- Usability  
- Structure  

It must serve both **technical and non-technical users**, balancing functionality with ease of use.

## Design Direction

The redesign should aim for:

- A modern **SaaS-style interface**  
- Clear information hierarchy  
- Strong usability across key workflows  
- Minimal clutter  
- A professional and trustworthy feel  

---

## Key Considerations

Designers should ensure:

- A smooth and efficient experience for regular use  
- Logical and easy-to-understand navigation  
- Layouts that support data-heavy or action-based pages without feeling overwhelming  
- A UI that feels **premium, modern, and trustworthy**
